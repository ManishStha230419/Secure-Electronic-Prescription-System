About the System

The Nepal E-Prescription System is a state-of-the-art electronic prescription management platform designed for Nepal's healthcare infrastructure.

Key Features:
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Digital signatures
  • Role-based access control
  • Complete audit trail
  • Real-time notifications
  • Advanced analytics
  • Mobile-responsive
  • Multi-user support
