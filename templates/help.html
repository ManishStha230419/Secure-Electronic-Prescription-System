Getting Started

Welcome to Nepal E-Prescription System! This secure platform enables electronic prescription management with military-grade encryption.

  1. Click "Create" in the navigation menu
  2. Select the patient from the dropdown
  3. Fill in medication details (name, dosage, quantity)
  4. Add diagnosis and special instructions
  5. Click "Create & Encrypt Prescription"
  6. The prescription is automatically encrypted with AES-256-GCM

  1. Go to "Prescriptions" to see all active prescriptions
  2. Click "View" on the prescription to dispense
  3. The system automatically decrypts using your RSA private key
  4. Verify prescription details and signature
  5. Click "Dispense Prescription"

Your data is protected with 8 cryptographic algorithms:
  • RSA-2048: Public-key encryption
  • AES-256-GCM: Data encryption
  • RSA-PSS: Digital signatures
  • SHA-256: Hash function
  • X.509 v3: Certificate structure
  • OAEP, PSS, MGF1: Padding algorithms
Common Issues
Problem: Cannot see decrypted prescription
Solution: Only pharmacists and patients can decrypt prescriptions.
Problem: Account locked
Solution: After 5 failed login attempts, account is locked for 30 minutes.
